Welcome to Atlantis The World School

Atlantis The World School is a K-12 School established to match the standards of the best global schools in the country. It is founded by Komamdur Educational Society, the organization that has striven to ensure future of the youth of the region by establishing several education institutions of merit. The school is founded envisioning a generation equipped with 21st century skills and aptitudes of global significance. With infrastructure and instructional facilities that are designed to support all-round child development and curriculum that is embedded in model pedagogical perspectives. The school is international in outlook set to prove as an asset in the educational parlance.

The school intends to herald an era of international education in the region and broadenes the dimensions of school education by integrating it with skill and personality development.
